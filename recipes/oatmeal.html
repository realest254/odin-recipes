Oatmeal recipe

Description

Oatmeal is a healthy and easy to cook meal. It takes about 5 minutes to cook and requires little to no experience to get started cooking. The ingredients are also easy to source and you can add any desired toppings to your liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oats
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • Pinches of cinnamon, nutmeg, and/or cardamom, optional

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a medium pot.
  2. Add the oats and salt and reduce heat to medium-low.
  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 15 to 20 minutes, or until thickened.
  4. Stir in cinnamon, nutmeg, or cardamom, if desired.
  5. Remove from heat and let stand for two minutes.
  6. Serve hot with desired toppings and honey or maple syrup, if desired.
